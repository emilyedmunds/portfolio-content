Upsilon Pi Epsilon - Michigan Iota

International Honors Society for the Computing and Information Disciplines

Upsilon Pi Epsilon (UPE) is the oldest international honor society for the computing and information disciplines. The Iota chapter of Upsilon Pi Epsilon aims to recognize academic excellence, share research, present scholarship and networking opportunities, and connect students in the computing and information disciplines through academic and social events on campus, while creating lifelong bonds.

UPE has received endorsements from the two largest computer organizations in the world, the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) and and the IEEE Computer Society (IEEE-CS). UPE is also a charter member of The International Federation of Engineering Education Societies (IFEES).
